# Ingredients

* 1tbsp butter
* 1 egg
* 1/3 cup (40g) flour
* 1/2 cup (125g) milk
* salt
* sugar

# Recipe

1. add 1tbsp of butter to a pan on medium heat
2. add the flour to a bowl
3. add the egg
4. add a little dash of salt
5. add a bigger dash of sugar
6. add a little bit of the milk (adding only a little bit now prevents clumps from forming)
7. mix it
8. once everything is incorporated and smooth, add the rest of the milk
9. add the melted butter from the pan to the batter and mix it
10. add a bit of batter to one side of the pan, angle the pan after to spread it evenly
11. once it has set and got a nice browning, flip the crêpe
12. cook for roughly 30 seconds on the other side, after that the crêpe is done
13. optionally: the side that first touched the pan is usually the nice side, make sure that becomes the outer side later when you fold the crêpe
