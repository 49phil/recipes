# Ingredients

* 2 eggs
* 2 egg yolks
* 5tbsp butter
* 3tbsp sugar
* 3.5oz (100g) dark chocolate
* 3tbsp flour
* 4tsp good cocoa powder
* 1/8tsp vanilla
* salt

# Recipe

1. butter the inside of four 5 1/2 ounce ramekins
2. add eggs, egg yolks and sugar in a bowl together
3. mix them until foamy and lighter in color
4. melt chocolate and butter together
5. stir the chocolate mixture into the bowl until combined
6. sift in cocoa powder and stir until combined
7. sift in flour and salt, stir until combined
8. stir in vanilla
9. transfer the batter to the ramekins
    1. easier by using a resealable plastic bag and cutting off one corner
    2. gently tap the ramekins to remove air bubbles
10. refrigerate for 30 minutes
11. preheat oven to 425F (220C)
12. add the ramekins to a casserole dish
13. pour warm tap water into casserole dish until it reaches halway up the sides of the ramekins
14. bake for 15-18 minutes
15. let cool for 15 minutes
16. remove from ramekins using a knife to loosen the edges
