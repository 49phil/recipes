# Ingredients

* guanciale (or pancetta in a pinch)
* white wine
* peeled tomatoes (crushed)
* peperoncino (or chili flakes) or black pepper
* pasta
* pecorino cheese

# Recipe

1. cut the meat in thick pieces
2. cook the meat (without olive oil or butter) and render out the fat
3. add a splash of white wine and deglaze the pan
4. add the tomato sauce, peperoncino (or chili flakes) and let it cook gently
5. cook the pasta in a pot
6. slightly before the pasta is done, add it to the sauce pan and finish it there
7. add pasta water depending on the thickness of the sauce
8. add pecorino cheese bit by bit instead of all at once and wait until you have incorporated it before adding more
9. garnish with more pecorino cheese
