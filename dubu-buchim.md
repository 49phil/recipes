# Ingredients

* 400g tofu
* 3tbsp vegetable oil
* 2tbsp soy sauce
* 2tbsp gochugaru
* 1 clove of garlic
* 2tbsp finely chopped onion
* 2 green onions chopped
* 2tbsp toasted sesame oil
* 1/2tsp sugar
* 1tbsp toasted sesame seeds

# Recipe

1. combine sauce
    1. add soy sauce
    2. add gochugaru
    3. add minced garlic
    4. add chopped onion
    5. add chopped green onion
    6. add sesame oil
    7. add sugar
2. cut tofu into equal pieces, about half a cm thick
3. dry tofu with a paper towel
4. add vegetable oil to a pan
5. set to medium-high heat
6. add tofu to skillet and make sure they don't stick to each other
7. turn down heat to medium
8. cook for 5 minutes or until bottom turns crunchy and golden brown
9. flip and cook on the other side for another 5 minutes or until crunchy and golden brown
10. transfer cooked tofu to a plate
11. drizzle sauce evenly over each piece
12. sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds
