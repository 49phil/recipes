# Ingredients

* rice
* half onion
* ground pork
  * soy sauce
  * sugar
  * sesame oil
  * minced garlic
  * sesame seeds
  * black pepper
* mushrooms
* vegetable oil
* 1 egg
* bibimbap sauce
  * 4tbsp gochujang paste
  * 2tbsp mirin
  * 2tbsp rice vinegar
  * 1.5tsp soy sauce
  * 3tsp white sugar
  * 1 clove of garlic, grated
  * 2.5tsp toasted sesame oil

# Recipe

1. cook rice and prepare the sauce
2. marinate the meat
    1. add soy sauce
    2. add sesame oil
    3. add sugar
    4. add garlic
    5. add black pepper
3. add vegetable oil to a pan
4. fry the veggies one after the other and set aside
5. fry the meat and set aside
6. fry the egg and set aside
7. add cooked rice to a bowl
8. add the egg in the middle
9. add the fried ingredients, one into each corner
10. add sauce on top and mix everything together
