# Ingredients

* pork loin
* flour
* 3 eggs
* bread crumbs
* salt
* black pepper
* oil
* cabbage
* tonkatsu sauce

# Recipe

1. thinly slice cabbage
2. cut the pork loin in 1in thick slices
3. pound out the pork until it is 1/4 of an inch thick
4. make litte incisions across the pork loin
5. prepare 3 plates for the breading station
    1. a plate of flour
    2. break the 3 eggs in another plate and mix them
    3. a plate of bread crumbs
6. add a pork loin to the flour, then the egg wash and last to the bread crumbs
7. heat a pot of oil to around 350F or 176C
8. fry for 2 to 3 minutes until light golden brown
9. after all of them are down, crank the heat to 375F (190C) to give them a double fry for another minute
10. serve with the thinly sliced cabbage and tonkatsu sauce
