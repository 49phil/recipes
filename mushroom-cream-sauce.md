# Ingredients

* 250g or 400g of mushrooms
* heavy cream (15-35% of fat is ok)
* 5 garlic cloves
* olive oil
* black pepper
* salt
* 1/2 cup (125g) of parmigiano reggiano

# Recipe

1. clean the mushrooms with a paper towel
2. cut the hard stem off of them
3. cut them into fine slices
4. mince 5 cloves of garlic
5. add olive oil to a pan and add the minced garlic and let it cook at medium heat
6. add some black pepper and salt to it
7. after about 30 to 60 seconds, add the mushrooms to it
8. let the mushrooms cook and give out their water
9. once the mushrooms are cooked, add the heavy cream and let it incorporate
10. add the parmigiano reggiano
11. season to taste
