# Ingredients

* 1cup (120g) all purpose flour
* 1 1/4tsp baking powder
* 1/2tsp fine salt
* 2tbsp corn starch (or rice flour/potato starch)
* 1cup lager-style beer

# Recipe

1. make a cup of self rising flour
    1. add 1cup (120g) of all purpose flour
    2. add 1/2tsp fine salt
    3. add 2tbsp corn starch (or rice flour/potato starch)
    4. add 1/4tsp baking powder
2. make sure the cod is as dry as possible and lightly dust it with corn starch
3. add the beer to the flour and mix in to get the consistency you want (the thicker the batter, the thicker the coating once it's fried)
4. coat the cod in the beer batter
5. fry it in neutral cooking oil for about 3 to 4 minutes or until golden brown
6. about halfway through you can flip them over
