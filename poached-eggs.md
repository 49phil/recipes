# Ingredients

* 2 eggs (or how many you want to make)
* optional: white vinegar (will help the poached eggs hold its shape better)

# Recipe

1. bring a pot of water to a slight boil
2. OPTIONALLY add a splash of white vinegar
3. crack an egg into a little bowl
4. use a big spoon to create a vortex in the middle of the pot
5. slowly add the cracked egg from the bowl into the pot
6. boil the egg for 3 to 5 minutes depending on how runny you want the inside
