# Ingredients

* broccoli
* steam basket
* optionally: ice cubes

# Recipe

1. bring a pot with a bit of water in the bottom and a steam basket on top of that to a boil
2. add the broccoli and put on a lid
3. after 3-4 minutes, the broccoli is done
4. optionally: if you need the broccoli a bit later, immediately add it to a bowl with ice water, this will stop the broccoli from turning brown
    1. once you need it again, reheat the broccoli in a pan sauce you are making for example
