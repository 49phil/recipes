# Ingredients

* 6 cloves garlic
* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 1tsp red pepper flakes
* 1/4 cup parsley parsley
* 1lb (450g) pasta
* salt
* black pepper

# Recipe

1. start boiling the water
2. slice the garlic as thin as possible
3. chop the parsley
4. add pasta to boiling water
5. add olive oil into a pan
6. add garlic once the oil is shimmering
7. once it starts to brown, add red pepper flakes and turn off heat
8. when the garlic is golden brown, add pasta and a little bit of pasta water to emulsify the sauce
9. mix in the parsley
10. add salt and black pepper
