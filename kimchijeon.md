# Ingredients

* 1/2lb (225g) well fermented napa cabbage kimchi
* 2tbsp kimchi brine
* 3 scallions chopped
* 1/2tsp sugar
* 1/2cup flour
* 1/2cup water
* 4tbsp vegetable oil

# Recipe

1. Combine kimchi, kimchi brine, scallions, sugar, flour and water in a bowl
2. mix with a spoon
3. heat a 12in (30cm) skillet over medium heat
4. add 2tbsp of vegetable oil
5. pour the batter into the pan
6. spread it with the spoon to form a circle
7. cook until bottom is golden crisp (3 to 5 minutes)
8. turn it over and drizzle the remaining 2tbsp vegetable oil under the pancake
9. cook again for 3 to 5 minutes until bottom is golden crips
10. flip one more time and cook for 1 more minute
