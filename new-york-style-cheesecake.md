# Ingredients

* 18 graham crackers (or around 240g)
* 3tbsp melted butter
* 1cup (240g) sour cream
* 1/4cup flour
* 1tbsp vanilla extract
* 4 (8oz/227g) packages cream cheese
* 1 1/2cups white sugar
* 2/3cup milk
* 4 eggs
* 1tsp finely grated lemon zest
* 1tsp finely grated orange zest

# Recipe

1. mix graham crackers and melted butter
2. lightly grease the bottom and sides of a 9in (23cm) springform pan
3. press crumb mixture into the bottom of the springform pan and about 1/2in (1.27cm) up the side
4. whisk sour cream, flour and vanilla extract in a bowl
5. add cream cheese and sugar to a separate bowl
6. stir together with a spoon until incorporated
7. whisk eggs in one at a time
8. stir in lemon zest, orange zest and sour cream mixture until incorporated
9. preheat oven to 350F (175C)
10. bake in the preheated oven for around 1 hour until the surface of the cheesecake is firm except for a small spot in the center that will jiggle
11. turn off the oven
12. let it cool in the oven for 3 to 4 hours
