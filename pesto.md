# Ingredients

* 1 clove of garlic
* 1 teaspoon of salt
* 1/4 cup (30g) of pine nuts
* 1/4 cup (30g) of basil
* 1/3 cup (43g) of olive oil
* 10-30g of grated parmegiano regiano

# Recipe

Traditionally done with a mortar and pestle, but you can also use a food processor.

1. add the clove of garlic, a teaspoon of salt and the pine nuts and mash/mix them together
2. add the basil, remove them from the stem before and mash/mix it in
3. add a little splash of olive oil
4. add the cheese and process
5. add the rest of the olive oil
6. optionally: if the pesto breaks, add a tablespoon of hot water and blitz it in a food processor
7. continually mix and eat it right away after making it (otherwise, add it to a container with an oil layer on top and put it in the fridge)
