# Ingredients

* skinless salmon filets
* flour
* pepper
* garlic powder
* unsalted butter
* parsley
* vegetable stock
* 2 garlic cloves
* optionally: lemon juice

# Recipe

1. mix the flour with pepper, garlic powder and salt
2. add the salmon to the flour mixture
3. fry them in a pan with butter until they've developed a nice crust
4. put them out, we will continue cooking them later
5. fry some minced garlic in the pan the salmon was just in
6. add vegetable stock
7. add some more butter
8. add fresh parsley
9. optionally: if needed, add more butter
10. add the salmons again and bask them in the sauce
11. optionally: add some lemon juice
