# Ingredients

* 100g sour cream
* 3tbsp of vinegar
* 1tsp of salt
* stevia
* salad

# Recipe

1. add sour cream to a bowl
2. add the vinegar
3. add the salt
4. add a little bit of stevia as a sweetener
5. mix everything and check if it needs more stevia or salt
6. add water to adjust consistency if it is to thick
7. combine with salad and mix everything together
