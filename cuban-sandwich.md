# Ingredients

* 1/4cup mayonnaise
* 1/4cup mustard
* 1pinch cayenne pepper
* loaf of cuban bread (alternatively, something like ciabatta is also possible)
* 8 slices swiss cheese
* 6 thin slices smoked fully-cooked ham
* 1 1/2cups cooked [pulled pork](paper-pork.md)
* 1 large dill pickle sliced thinly lengthwise
* 2tbsp butter

# Recipe

1. add mayonnaise, mustard and a pinch of cayenne into a bowl and mix
2. slice loaf in half
3. split the halves open evenly
4. apply the sauce on both sides
5. add a couple of slices of cheese
6. add a couple of slices of ham
7. add pork, make sure it is heated
8. add dill pickle
9. add a couple of slices of cheese
10. add butter into a pan over medium heat
11. once the butter is melted, add the prepared sandwich to the pan and toast for 3 to 4 minutes per side, pressing it down with something heavy like another pan
