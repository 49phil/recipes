# Ingredients

* 5tbsp milk or heavy cream
* 1tsp lemon zest
* 1/2tsp fine salt
* pinch of nutmeg
* pinch of cayenne
* 4 slices white bread (toast)
* 8 thin slices melting cheese (cheddar/swiss/gruyere/havarti etc.)
* 4oz (113g) thinly sliced baked ham
* 2tbsp butter
* 2 eggs for the batter
* 2 [poached eggs](poached-eggs.md) on top

# Recipe

1. make the batter
    1. 5tbsp of milk or heavy cream depending on how rich you want it
    2. 2 eggs
    3. 1/2tsp fine salt
    4. a pinch of cayenne
    5. a pinch of nutmeg
    6. 1tsp lemon zest
2. mix it up with a fork until it is runny
3. give the slices of white bread a light toasting
4. add ham and cheese to the bread
5. press the two halves together
6. dip the bread into the batter until it cannot soak in anymore
7. add 2tbsp of butter to a pan at medium heat (or lower, depending on your stove)
8. fry the bread in the butter until it is heated through and the cheese is melty
9. while it is frying, make 2 poached eggs and add them on top
10. garnish the poached eggs with a pinch of cayenne
