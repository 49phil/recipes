# Ingredients

* 350g silken tofu
* 2 cloves of garlic
* half a yellow onion
* 3 scallions
* 150g kimchi
* 1tbsp gochujang
* 2tbsp vegetable oil
* 1tbsp sesame oil
* 2tbsp soy sauce
* 475ml vegetable stock / alternatively water
* 1 egg

# Recipe

1. finely chop garlic
2. chop scallion and leave half of it for garnish later
3. put a pot on medium to high heat
4. add vegetable and sesame oil
5. add garlic, gochujang and kimchi
6. cook for 5 minutes or until the gochujang starts to stick to the bottom
7. add soy sauce and stock
8. bring to a simmer and then set to low
9. let it simmer until the ingredients are cooked through
10. add tofu and optionally mushrooms you have
11. slowly chop up the tofu and let it all cook for another 3 minutes
12. take the pot off the heat and crack an egg into it
13. garnish with scallions
