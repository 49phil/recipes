# Ingredients

* 2 large chicken breasts
* 400g butter milk
* 2g onion powder
* 7g garlic powder
* 5g paprika
* 25g salt
* 300g all purpose flour
* 3g black pepper
* 400 to 500g of neutral oil (e.g. canola)

# Recipe

1. poke holes in the chicken (you can use a fork for this or a jaccard)
2. cut chicken into strips that are 1in wide
3. cut strips into 1in cubes
4. make butter milk brine
    1. add butter milk
    2. add 2g onion powder
    3. add 2g garlic powder
    4. add 2g paprika
    5. add 20g of salt
    6. whisk together until salt is dissolved
5. add cubed meat into the brine
6. stir until all pieces are coated
7. move brine into the fridge for 25 minutes
8. make breading
    1. add flour
    2. add 5g of salt
    3. add 3g of black pepper
    4. add 3g of paprika
    5. add 5g of garlic powder
    6. whisk together
9. add 75 to 100g of butter milk to the breading
10. whisk together to get some hydrated clumps
11. toss everything together
12. add a couple of cubes to the flour mixture
13. lightly toss the nuggets to coat them
14. lay the nuggets out on a baking sheet
15. add oil to a sauce pan
16. shallow fry the nuggets at 350F (175C) for about 7 minutes
