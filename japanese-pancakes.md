# Ingredients

* 2 large eggs
* 2tbsp (30g) milk
* 3tbsp (22.5g) flour
* 2tbsp (25g) sugar
* 1/4tsp baking powder
* 1/2tsp vanilla extract
* 1tsp vegetable oil

# Recipe

1. separate egg white and egg yolk
2. add 2tbsp milk to the egg yolk and mix together
3. add baking powder to flour and mix
4. sift flour/baking powder mixture into egg yolk
5. add vanilla extract to the egg yolk
6. start preheating a pan over the lowest setting (this will depend a lot on your stovetop)
7. add 1/3 of sugar to the egg white and start whisking
8. continue adding the rest of the sguar while mixing
9. whisk until stiff peaks form
10. add meringue to egg yolk and carefully fold it in
11. add a little bit of vegetable oil to the pan and soak up the excess with a tissue
12. add batter to the pan and cover with a lid
13. cook for about 3 to 5 minutes or until the bottom is golden brown
    1. optionally: add a few water droplets to the pan to cook the pancakes faster
14. gently flip the pancakes and put the lid back on
15. cook them for another 3 to 5 minutes
