# Ingredients

* 1lb (436g) of blueberries
* 1/2cup white sugar
* 1tbsp lemon juice
* 2tsp lemon zest
* 1 1/2tsp cornstarch
* 1 large egg
* 1tsp water
* 1tbsp demerara sugar
* 1 1/2cups all purpose flour
* 1/2cup cornmeal
* 1/2tsp fine salt
* 1/2cup colt sunsalted butter, cubed
* 6tbsp ice water or as needed

# Recipe

1. combine flour, cornmeal and salt in a bowl
2. add cold butter and combine with a pastry blender until coarse crumbs form
3. drizzle in cold water and combine with a fork
4. when the dough comes together, turn it out onto a work surface and bring it together with your hands
5. press dough into a disk and wrap in plastic wrap
6. chill the dough in the refrigerator for 30 minutes
7. combine blueberries, sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest and cornstarch in a bowl
8. after the dough has chilled for 30 minutes, roll it out on a lightly floured work surface into a 15 inch circle, 1/8 inch thick
9. place the dough on a baking sheet
10. add blueberry filling to the center of the dough, leaving 3 to 4 inches of space around the border of the dough
11. fold dough up and around the berries, overlapping each fold that came before
12. mix beaten egg and water in a bowl
13. brush over egg wash on the dough folds
14. sprinkle demerara sugar on the brushed dough
15. bake in the center of a pre-heated oven at 400F (205C) for about 45 minutes
