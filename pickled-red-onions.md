# Ingredients

* 1 red onion
* white wine vinegar
* salt

# Recipe

1. cut the red onion in half
2. then cut it root to stem
3. mixture equal parts water and vinegar and add a nice pinch of salt
4. bringt hat mixture to a boil
5. add the onions to a glass container that you can seal
6. when the mixture is boiling, pour it over the onions in the glass
7. seal it and invert the glass container so it develops a nice seal
8. once it's cooled down, put it in the fridge so it can pickle
