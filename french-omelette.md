# Ingredients

* 4 eggs (for a roughly 10inch/25cm pan)
* salt
* butter
* optional: parsley
* optional: chives

# Recipe

1. crack the 4 eggs into a bowl
2. add a bit of salt
3. beat the egg
4. optionally: cut some chives for later
5. optionally: add parsley to the egg mixture
6. add a few drops of water to the pan (high heat, probably more mid to high on induction)
7. once it has evaporated, add butter to the pan
8. onces the butter is melted but not browning, add the egg
9. start shaking the pan with your left hand and stir with your right hand (use a fork)
10. this should cook for around a minute
11. once its relatively even, use the fork to gently start folding the omelette
12. to fold over the last bit onto itself, hit the pan where it meets the handle so that the omelette gets pushed further to the end
13. then just fold the top over so that it's tucked in
14. angle the pan towards a plate so that the current top side of the omelette becomes the bottom of the omelette on the plate
15. optionally: add chives on top
