# Ingredients

* 2tbsp kosher salt
* 1tbsp freshly ground black pepper
* 1tbsp smoked paprika
* 2tsp onion powder
* 1tsp garlic powder
* 1tbsp brown sugar
* 1tsp cayenne
* pork shoulder
* parchment paper
* aluminum foil

# Recipe

1. add the seasoning into a bowl and mix
2. apply seasoning on the pork shoulder
3. place pork on parchment paper and roll it up as tight as possible
4. place it sideways on another parchment paper and roll it up again
5. wrap it in aluminum foil as tight as possible
6. wrap it two more times in aluminum foil
7. place in a roasting pan
8. bake at 225F (110C) for 1.75 hours per pound of pork
9. after taking it out, let it side for at least an hour before unwrapping
