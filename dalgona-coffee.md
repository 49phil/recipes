# Ingredients

* instant coffee (ratio 1)
* water (ratio 3)
* sugar (ratio 3)
* milk
* ice cubes

So around 6g of instant coffee means roughly 20g of water and sugar.

# Recipe

1. add the instant coffee to a bowl
2. add sugar
3. add water
4. whisk (easier using a hand mixer, especially when making large batches)
5. once it's soft and gooey, it's done
6. add ice cubes to a cup
7. add milk
8. add the dalgona coffee on top
