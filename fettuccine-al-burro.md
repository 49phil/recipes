# Ingredients

* 5 ounces (141g) of unsalted butter
* 5 ounces (141g) of parmigiano reggiano
* 200g of fettucine
* NOT TRADITIONAL: black pepper

# Recipe

1. start cooking the fettucine
2. heat a bowl or alternatively, soften the butter
3. add 5 ounces of unsalted butter
4. toss the cooked fettucine into the bowl and combine with the butter
5. add 2 1/2 (70.5g) ounces of grated parmigiano reggiano to the bowl and combine
6. add another 2 1/2 (70.5) ounces of grated parmigiano reggiano on top and incorporate it
7. add 1/4 cup of starchy cooking water or as much as needed
8. toss everything around with a pinch of salt
9. NOT TRADITIONAL: add black pepper
10. add more grated parmesan on top
