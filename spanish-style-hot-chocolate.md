# Ingredients

* 2 1/4cups whole milk (3.5% fat)
* 4oz (113g) 70% dark chocolate for the thin version and 6oz (170g) for the thick version
* 2tsp white sugar
* 1 pinch of kosher salt
* 1/2tsp cornstarch for the thin version and 1tsp cornstarch for the thick version
* optionally: cayenne pepper to garnish

# Recipe

1. break up chocolate
2. add whole milk to sauce pan
3. add white sugar
4. add pinch of salt
5. add cornstarch
6. whisk together
7. place over medium-high heat
8. until milk starts to simmer (be careful that the milk does not scorch onto the bottom)
9. whisk continuously
10. once the milk starts to simmer, remove from heat
11. add dark chocolate and whisk until it comes together
12. optionally: add cayenne pepper as garnish
