# Ingredients

* 1/2tsp of dijon mustard
* 1cup of mayoannaise
* 2tbsp of chopped dill
* 1tbsp of chopped parsley
* 2tsp of chopped tarragon
* 2tbsp of capers
* 1/4cup pickles
* 1 lemon
* cayenne pepper

# Recipe

1. add the mayonnaise to a bowl
2. add the dill, parsley and tarragon
3. drain and finely chop the capers and add them
4. finely chop the pickles and add them
5. add 1/2tsp of dijon mustard
6. add the juice of one lemon (or as much for the consistency you want)
7. refrigerate for up to 3 days
8. when serving, add some cayenne pepper on top
