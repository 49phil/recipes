# Ingredients

* 1 cucumber
* 1/4cup thinly sliced onion
* 1 green onion
* 2 garlic cloves
* 2tbsp soy sauce
* 2tsp gochugaru
* 2tsp sesame oil
* 2tsp toasted sesame seeds
* 1tsp sugar

# Recipe

1. cut the cucumber lengthwise and then diagonally into slices
2. put the cucumber into a large bowl
    1. add onion
    2. add green onion
    3. add minced garlic
    4. add soy sauce
    5. add gochugaru
    6. add sesame oil
    7. add sugar
3. mix until sugar is dissolved
