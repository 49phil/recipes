# Ingredients

* 6 eggs
* 250g sugar + 2tbsp of sugar
* 1 pinch of salt
* 125g flour
* 80g ground almonds for biscuit base
* 60g corn starch
* 1tsp backing powder
* 60g butter
* 200g of jam
* 1kg marzipan raw mass
  * 200g for filling
  * 800g for outside
* 900ml heavy cream
  * 600ml for filling
  * 300ml for decorating top
* 3 pack cream stiffener
  * 2 for filling
  * 1 for outside
* 50g ground almonds or hazel nuts for filling
* 10tbsp milk

# Recipe

1. add eggs, sugar and salt to a mixing bowl and whisk together for about 20 minutes
2. add dry ingredients through a siff
    1. add flour
    2. add corn starch
    3. backing powder
    4. ground almonds
3. slowly fold the ingredients into the mixture
4. add melted butter and whisk it in
5. add the batter to a springform and bake at 175C for 35 to 40 minutes
6. for the filling, add the ingredients to a bowl
    1. add marzipan cut into small pieces
    2. add 5tbsp milk
7. press the marzipan into the milk to mix the two
8. add the rest of the milk (5tbsp) and whisk it together
9. add heavy cream and cream stiffener to a separate bowl and whisk until stiff
10. add marzipan milk mixture from before and slowly fold it in
11. add ground almonds/hazel nuts and fold those in as well
12. take the base and cut it into 3 even pieces
13. alternate layer of base, jam and filling
16. for decorating the top
    1. add 300ml heavy cream
    2. 1pack cream stiffener
    3. add 2tbsp of sugar
17. whisk until stiff and coat the top and sides with it and hold onto the excess
14. roll out marzipan raw mass until you have enough to cover the top and sides of the cake
15. place the marzipan over the cake and lightly press it in at the bottom, cutting of the excess
16. use the excess cream from coating the top and sides of the cake to further decorate the top to your liking
