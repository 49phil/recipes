# Ingredients

* half an onion
* 3 ounces or a little more than 1/3 cup (85g) of tomato paste
* olive oil
* salt
* 2 cloves of garlic
* red pepper flakes
* heavy cream
* parmigiano reggiano

# Recipe

1. dice the onion
2. mince the garlic
3. add olive oil to pan
4. add the onion to the pan and cook for 3 to 4 minutes
5. add the garlic to the pan and cook for 30 seconds
6. add chili flakes
7. add 85g of tomato paste
8. bring down the heat a little
9. add three ounces (77g) of heavy cream
10. add one ounce (28g) of vodka
11. allow to cook for no more than a minute
12. once pasta is one minute before desired level of doneness add the pasta to the sauce
13. add the pasta water to the sauce
14. kill the heat
15. add parmesan
