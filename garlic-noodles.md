# Ingredients

* 1 1/2tbsp soy sauce
* 1tbsp oyster sauce
* 1 1/2tsp worcestershire sauce
* 1tsp asian fish sauce
* 1/4tsp sesame oil
* cayenne pepper
* 4tbsp unsalted butter
* 8 cloves finely minced garlic
* 1/4cup finely grated parmigiano reggiano
* red chili flakes

# Recipe

1. mince the garlic and reserve it for cooking
2. combine the sauce
    1. soy sauce
    2. oyster sauce
    3. worcestershire sauce
    4. asian fish sauce
    5. sesame oil
    6. add a pinch of cayenne pepper
3. melt the unsalted butter in a pan on medium heat
4. once the butter starts to bubble, add the garlic
5. only stir it for about a minute, do not let the garlic brown
6. add the sauce to the pan
7. once its incorporated, turn off the heat
8. cook noodles until tender
9. finish them in the pan with the sauce
10. add parmigiano reggiano on top and incorporate
11. garnish with red chili flakes
