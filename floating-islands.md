# Ingredients

* 2tsp vanilla extract
* 1/3 cup heavy cream
* 1/3 cup whole milk
* 1 egg
* pinch of salt
* 4tbsp white sugar
  * 2tbsp for floating islands
  * 2tbsp for creme anglaise

# Recipe

1. prepare creme anglaise in a saucepan
    1. add cream
    2. add milk
    3. add vanilla
2. separate egg into egg white and reserve egg yolk in the refrigerator until needed
3. add a tiny pinch of salt to the egg white and whisk until white and foamy
4. whisk in 1/2tbsp sugar at a time for a total of 2tbsp
5. whisk until stiff peaks form
6. use 2 large spoons to form floating islands into ovals
7. place creme anglaise in saucepan over medium-high heat and bring to a simmer
8. afterwards, reduce heat to lowest setting
9. place meringues into saucepan and cover with a lid
10. let them simmer for 12 minutes
11. after 12 minutes, turn off heat and transfer meringues to a cooling rack
12. add 2tbsp of sugar to creme anglaise
13. take the egg yolk from earlier and stir 3tbsp of creme anglaise mixture into it
14. then whisk tempered egg yolk into creme anglaise mixture
15. turn heat to medium-low
16. cook until mixture has thickened, then strain it into a bowl
17. chill creme anglaise in the refrigerator until needed
