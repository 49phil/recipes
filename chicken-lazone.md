# Ingredients

* 1 1/2tsp chili powder
* 1 1/2tsp smoked paprika
* 1/4tsp cayenne pepper
* 1/2tsp ground black pepper
* 2tsp garlic powder
* 1 1/2tsp onion powder
* salt
* 1/2 lemon
* 2/3 cup of heavy cream
* 1tbsp cold butter
* 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
* 1tbsp vegetable oil

# Recipe

1. Prepare the splice blend consisting of
    1. chili powder
    2. smoked paprika
    3. cayenne pepper
    4. ground black pepper
    5. garlic powder
    6. onion pwder
2. season the chicken with salt on both sides
3. season the chicken with the splice blend on both sides, use the rest of the spices for the sauce later on
4. Add the vegetable oil to a pan on medium high heat
5. Add the chicken and cook it for 3 to 4 minutes (depending on thickness) on each side
6. turn off heat
7. add the spice blend to the pan and cook for roughly 30 seconds
8. add the juice of half a lemon to the pan
9. turn heat back to medium high
10. add heavy cream
11. once the sauce simmers, reduce to medium low
12. add the chicken back into the pan and finish cooking them through
13. add water or cream to thin the sauce
