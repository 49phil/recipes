# Ingredients

* 1lb (450g) pork
* 1lb (450g) beef
* 1/4tsp all spice
* 1/4tsp nutmeg
* salt
* 3/4tsp black pepper
* 1/2 yellow onion
* 4tbsp butter
* 1/3cup breadcrumbs
* 2 large eggs
* 3tbsp all-purpose flour
* 3 1/4cups beef broth
* 1/2cup heavy cream
* 1/2tsp sugar
* 1/4tsp worcestershire sauce

# Recipe

1. dice an onion
2. add 2tbsp of butter to a pan and cook the diced onion on medium heat until translucent
3. dump the onions in a bowl and keep the pan with the remaining butter for later
4. add milk, eggs and breadcrumbs and mix until combined
5. add nutmeg
6. add all spice
7. add black pepper
8. add salt
9. mix and then add your ground beef + pork
10. mix with a spatula
11. cover and let it rest in the fridge for an hour
12. go back to the pan and add the rest of the butter to make the sauce
    1. add flour and whisk it in over medium heat
    2. add beef broth and whisk it in
    3. add cream and whisk it in
    4. increase heat to medium-high and bring to a simmer
    5. season with black pepper, salt and worcestershire sauce
    6. once it simmers, let it simmer for 7 to 8 minutes
13. form the meatballs into 1oz balls
14. add them to a baking sheet
15. bake at 425F (215C) for about 20 minutes
16. add them to the sauce and bask them
17. cook until the sauce has the desired consistency
