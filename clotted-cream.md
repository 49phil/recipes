# Ingredients

* 4 cups (1l) heavy cream (Sahne 30-35% fat)

# Recipe

1. pour the heavy cream into a shallow baking dish about 7 or 8 inch square
2. transfer to oven set to two-sided heat (ober unterhitze) at about 175-180F (80C)
3. bake for about 12 hours
4. let it cool to room temperature for about an hour
5. wrap it with plastic
6. put it in the fridge overnight
7. next morning lift up the solidified clotted cream on the top
8. pour the remaining liquid into a bowl (you can use this for biscuits)
9. pack the solidified rest into a container (you can mix the crust or leave it rustic)
