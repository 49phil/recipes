# Ingredients

* 1 1/2lb skinless, bonless chicken thighs or chicken breasts
* 1tbsp vegetable oil
* 2tsp kosher salt
* 2tsp garam masala
* 2tsp cumin
* 1tsp corianer
* 1tsp smoked paprika
* 1tsp turmeric
* 1/2tsp black pepper
* 1/4tsp cayenne pepper
* 1/8tsp cardamom
* 2tbsp clarified butter
* 1 onion
* 1/4cup tomato paste
* 4 cloves garlic
* 1tbsp ginger
* 1cup crushed tomatoes
* 13oz heavy milk (or equivalent)
* 1/2cup chicken broth
* 2tbsp fresh cilantro
* 1/2tsp red pepper flakes
* salt

# Recipe

1. finely chop the onion
2. finely grate ginger
3. mince the garlic
4. whisk together seasonings
    1. salt
    2. garam masala
    3. cumin
    4. coriander
    5. paprika
    6. turmeric
    7. black pepper
    8. cayenne pepper
    9. cardamom
5. drizzle vegetable oil over chicken
6. sprinkle seasonings over chicken and coat evenly
7. melt clarified butter in a large pan over high heat
8. cook chicken in it until browned on all sides, about 5 to 10 minutes
9. transfer chicken to a plate
10. stir onion in pan over medium to high heat
11. once the onion softens and turns translucent, stir in tomato paste
12. when the paste caramelizes, stir in garlic and ginger and cook for around a minute
13. pour crushed tomatoes into pan to deglaze
14. bring to a boil
15. pour in heavy cream and chicken broth
16. reduce heat to medium-low and let it simmer for about 15 minutes
17. stir chicken and its juices into sauce
18. add cilantro
19. add red pepper flakes
20. let it simmer for another 10 to 15 minutes
21. season with salt and black pepper
