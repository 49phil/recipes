# Ingredients

* cherry tomatoes
* salt
* black pepper
* olive oil
* 4 cloves of garlic
* pasta

# Recipe

1. mince the garlic
2. add olive oil to a pan and immediately start boiling pasta
3. add the garlic to the pan and make sure to not let it burn
4. add the cherry tomatoes
5. once the cherry tomatoes have broken up and formed a sauce, add salt and black pepper
6. add the pasta directly to the sauce
7. add some of the pasta water to get a creamy sauce and adjust consistency
8. optionally: add fresh parmesan
