# Ingredients

* 1lb (500g) of beef (combination of lean and fat) cut into 1 to 2inch cubes
* 1inch of ginger
* 2 onions
* 1 apple
* 1 clove of garlic
* 1 carrot
* salt
* black pepper
* 1 cup of red wine
* 36g of all purpose flour
* 2tsp of cumin
* 2tsp of coriander
* 2tsp of turmeric
* 1tsp of cardamom
* 1tsp of cinnamon
* 1tsp of chili powder
* 1/2tsp of nutmeg
* 1/2tsp of ground clove
* 1/4tsp of black pepper
* 1 bay leaf
* 1tsp of beef bouillon paste
* 1tbsp of worchestershire sauce
* 2tbsp of honey
* 2g of instant coffee
* 8g of dark chocolate
* 2tbsp of butter
* 110g plain yogurt

# Recipe

1. finely chop the 2 onions
2. peel the carrot and chop it
3. peel the apple and halve it
4. grate 1/2 apple
5. remove the skin from the ginger
6. grate the ginger
7. grate the clove of garlic
8. sear the beef in a pot
9. caramelize the onions in a pan
10. once a nice fond has formed, season the beef with salt and black pepper
12. then deglace the pot with the red wine
13. add 3 cups of water to the beef
14. add the carrot directly to the beef
15. once the onions have a nice brown color on all sides, add the rest of the vegetation to the onion pan
16. add the bay leaf to the beef
17. add the seasonings into a small pan to toast them under low heat
    1. 36g flour
    2. 2tsp of cumin
    3. 2tsp of coriander
    4. 2tsp of turmeric
    5. 1tsp of cardamom
    6. 1tsp of cinnamon
    7. 1tsp of chili powder
    8. 1/2tsp of nutmeg
    9. 1/2tsp of ground clove
    10. 1/4tsp of black pepper
18. take some of the liquid from the beef and set it aside
19. add the caramelized and sauteed vegetable mixture to the beef
20. deglace the onion pan with the set aside liquid
21. add that fond back to the beef
22. add the toasted flour seasoning mixture to the beef and stir to incorporate it
23. simmer for one hour on low heat and stir occasionally to make sure the bottom does not burn
24. after simmering, remove the bay leaf
25. add 1tsp of beef bouillon paste
26. add 1tbsp of worchestershire sauce
27. add 2tbsp of honey
28. add 2g of instant coffee
29. add 8g of dark chocolate
30. after incorporating everything, take it from the heat
31. add 2tbsp of butter
32. add 110g of plain yogurt
