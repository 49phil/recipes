# Ingredients

* 500 to 1000g of beef chuck roast
* 2tbsp vegetable oil
* 2tsp olive oil
* 2tbsp hungarian paprika
* 2tsp caraway seeds, crushed
* 1tsp ground black pepper
* 1tsp dried marjoram
* 1/2tsp ground thyme
* 1/2tsp cayenne pepper
* 4cups chicken broth
* 1/4cup tomato paste
* 3 cloves garlic
* 2tbsp balsamic vinegar
* 1tsp white sugar
* 1 bay leaf
* salt

# Recipe

1. cut the beef into little cubes if not already done and chop the onions
2. season the beef with salt and black pepper
3. heat vegetable oil in a pan over high heat and add beef to it
4. once the beef is browned on all sides, add it to a large stockpot and turn off the heat
5. return the pan to medium heat and add the onions to it
6. drizzle the olive oil over the onions
7. season the onions with 1/2tsp salt and cook them until softened
8. transfer the onions to the stockpot
9. combine seasonings in the pan
    1. paprika
    2. caraway seeds
    3. black pepper
    4. marjoram
    5. thyme
    6. cayenne pepper
10. toast them over medium heat for 3 minutes or until fragrant
11. deglaze with 1cup of chicken broth
12. transfer to the stockpot
13. add remaining ingredients to stockpot
    1. add 3cups of chicken broth
    2. add tomato paste
    3. add crushed garlic
    4. add balsamic vinegar
    5. add 1/2tsp salt
    6. add 1 bay leaf
19. place stockpot on high heat and bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1 1/2 to 2 hours
20. when a fork inserts easily into meat, it's done
