# Ingredients

* 1 1/3cups all-purpose flour
* 3tbsp cocoa powder
* 1/4tsp baking soda
* 1tsp baking powder
* 4tbsp (57g) softened butter
* 1cup white sugar
* 2 large eggs
* 3/4cup buttermilk
* 2tsp white vinegar
* 1tsp vanilla extract
* 1tsp food coloring

# Recipe

1. mix the dry ingredients in a bowl and set aside
    1. add flour
    2. add cocoa powder
    3. add baking soda
    4. add baking powder
2. add softened butter to another bowl
3. add white sugar
4. mix together with a hand mixer
5. add one egg and mix until it is incorporated
6. add second egg and mix that in completely
7. add buttermilk
8. add vanilla extract
9. add white vinegar
10. add food coloring
11. mix together with a hand mixer
12. add dry ingredients and mix with a whisk
13. add paper liners to muffin tin
14. fill the liners 3/4 of the way
15. bake in a 350F (175C) pre-heated oven for 20-25 minutes
16. when a toothpick inserted in the center of a cupcake comes out clean, they are done
17. optionally: frost them with [cream cheese frosting](cream-cheese-frosting.md)
