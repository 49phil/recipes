# Ingredients

* 4 eggs
* 1 tablespoon (7g) lemon juice / 1 lemon
* 1 teaspoon (4g) water
* 1/2 teaspoon (2g) sugar
* pinch of salt
* 3/4 of a cup plus one tablespoon (182g) of unsalted butter

# Recipe

1. only use the egg yolk from the eggs
2. put the egg yolk into the double boiler bowl
3. add 1 teaspoon of water
4. add 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
5. add 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
6. add a pinch of salt
7. slowly melt the butter and make sure it's only slightly warm, if it's hot it's gonna destroy the immersion when mixing later
8. bring a small pot filled with water to a boil (make sure the double boiler does not touch the water)
9. add the double boiler on top of the pot with the steaming water
10. continously whisk!!! never stop whisking or the immersion will break
11. after 30 seconds, take the bowl off the heat and continue whisking until it becomes a bit lighter in color
12. place it back on the heat and continue whisking until it becomes thicker (should only take a minute or two)
13. now slowly add the butter mixture and KEEP WHISKING
14. if it seems too thicken up too much, remove it from the heat and keep whisking and add a little bit of lemon juice or water
15. once all the butter is incorporated, the sauce hollandaise is done
