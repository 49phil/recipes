# Ingredients

* 2 cups all purpose flour
* 1 3/4 cups sugar
* 1 1/2tsp baking soda
* 1 cup (227g) butter
* 1/3 cup cocoa powder
* 1 cup water
* 1/2tsp fine salt
* 2 eggs
* 1/2 cup sour cream
* 1tsp vanilla extract

# Recipe

1. add dry ingredients to a mixing bowl and whisk them together
    1. add flour
    2. add sugar
    3. add baking soda
2. add butter, cocoa powder, water and salt to a saucepan
3. place saucepan over medium heat and stir until everything is dissolved
4. pour about half of it into the dry ingredients and whisk together
5. pour the other half and whisk until it is incorporated
6. whisk in one egg at a time
7. whisk in vanilla extract
8. whisk in sour cream
9. coat the inside of a bundt pan with butter/flour or nonstick spray
10. pour in batter and tap it a few times to remove bubbles
11. bake at 350F (175C) for 50min
12. the cake is done once a skewer inserted comes out clean
13. let it cool for 15min before removing from the form
