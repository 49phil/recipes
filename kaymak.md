# Ingredients

* 500ml milk
* 500ml heavy cream

# Recipe

1. combine the milk and heavy cream in a rice cooker
2. put the rice cooker in warm mode for 12 hours
3. after 12 hours, cover it and put in the fridge over night
4. cut the top layer into 3 pieces and roll them up
