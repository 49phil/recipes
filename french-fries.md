# Ingredients

* 900g potatoes
* salt
* neutral cooking oil (like canola, peanut etc.)
* optionally: white vinegar

# Recipe

1. peel the potatoes
2. cut them into the size you want
3. cook them in salted boiling water for 8 to 12 minutes depending on their size
    * optionally: spike with a tablespoon of white vinegar (this helps the potatoes retain their form)
4. let them cool to room temperature on a wire baking rack
5. fry them in 350F oil for about 2-4 minutes, we only want to achieve a crust since the fries are already cooked through
