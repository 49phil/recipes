# Ingredients

* 4 eggs
* 2tsp (10ml) milk
* salt
* black pepper
* 3tbsp (42g) Japanese mayonnaise
* Japanese milkbread (or any other bread if you want)

# Recipe

1. boil eggs for 10 to 12 minutes
2. once boiled, add them to an ice bath and let them cool
3. peel them once they cooled down
4. add the peeled eggs in a bowl and smash them with a fork
5. make sure to break up the whites
6. add mayonnaise
7. add milk
8. season with salt and freshly ground black pepper
9. mix it all together
10. add it on your bread
11. if you have Japanese milkbread, butter one side and toast it over medium heat
12. add egg salad on the untoasted side and press two halves together into a sandwich
