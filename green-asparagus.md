# Ingredients

* olive oil
* green asparagus
* parmigiano reggiano
* 1 lemon
* black pepper
* salt

# Recipe

1. add the asparagus into a pan with nothing in there to let them blister and dry out the skin (cook on high)
2. season with salt and pepper
3. now add the olive oil
4. sear asparagus for 45 seconds per side until blistered and colored
5. add parmesan
6. add lemon zest and remove from heat
