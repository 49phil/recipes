# Ingredients

* chicken (e.g. chicken thigh with skin on)
* salt
* black pepper
* corn starch
* teriyaki sauce
* sesame seeds

# Recipe

1. season the chicken with salt and black pepper (preferably letting it marinade over night in the fridge)
2. add the chicken to a cold pan and let the fat slowly render out
3. once the skin has some nice browning, add the teriyaki sauce
4. bask the chicken in it
5. add some roasted sesame seeds on top for serving
