# Ingredients

* 2cups (240g) flour
* 1tsp baking powder
* 1tsp baking soda
* 1tsp salt
* 6tbsp (85g) butter
* 1/3cup plain smooth peanut butter
* 1cup (200g) white sugar
* 2 large ripe bananas
* 2 eggs
* 2tbsp milk
* 1/4tsp vanilla extract
* 1/2cup chocolate chips

# Recipe

1. add flour, salt, baking powder and baking soda to a bowl, whisk and set aside
2. add butter, peanut butter and sugar to a different bowl
3. mix with a hand mixer
4. add bananas and beat again until light and fluffy
5. mix in eggs one at a time
6. add milk and vanilla
7. add flour mixture and mix on low speed until 75% incorporated
8. add chocolate chips
9. use spatula to fully incorporate flour and mix in chocolate chips
10. preheat oven to 350F (175C)
11. divide batter evenly between muffin cups (should come out as 12 muffins)
12. bake in the preheated oven for 23 to 25 minutes
13. when a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean, the muffins are done
