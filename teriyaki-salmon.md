# Ingredients

* salmon
* black pepper
* salt
* teriyaki sauce
* butter/oil
* flour/corn starch

# Recipe

1. season the salmon on both sides with salt and black pepper
2. cover the salmon in a light dust of flour or corn starch
3. cook the salmon in a bit of butter or oil (if the salmon has skin on it, cook it skin side down first)
4. after 4 minutes on one side, flip it
5. after 2 minutes, add the teriyaki sauce and bask the salmon in it
6. after 2 more minutes, the salmon is done
