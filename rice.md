# Ingredients

* rice (about 50 to 75g for a side dish portion)
* rice cooker

# Recipe

1. wash the rice until the water comes out clear
2. add the rice to a rice cooker
3. fill the rice cooker with as much water that when a finger touches the rice, it's submerged up until the first knuckle
