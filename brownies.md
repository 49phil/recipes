# Ingredients

* 2/3cup (85g) of flour
* 3/4tsp (6g) of kosher salt or about half of that if using fine salt
* 3 ounces (85g) of unsweetened chocolate
* 1/2cup (113g) of unsalted butter
* 1 1/4cups (250g) white sugar
* 2 large eggs
* 1tsp pure vanilla extract

# Recipe

1. preheat the oven to 350F (175C)
2. butter an 8inch square baking dish or pan and line it with parchment paper
3. mix flour and salt together in a bowl
4. chop the chocolate if not already done
5. mix chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl and set over a saucepan with gently simmering water and stir until smooth, then set aside
6. mix sugar and eggs in a bowl until very airy and thick on high speed (5 minutes)
7. mix in the vanilla extract
8. slowly drizzle in the chocolate mixture and mix on high speed
9. dump in the flour mixture and mix on low speed until the flour disappears
10. transfer the batter with a spatula into the baking dish spread evenly
11. bake until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean (roughly 30 minutes)
