# Ingredients

* [pesto](pesto.md)
* cherry tomatoes
* 2 garlic cloves
* ciabatta bread (or baguette if you are in a pinch)
* mozzarella cheese
* butter
* olive oil
* salt
* black pepper

# Recipe

1. cut open the ciabatta bread and spread butter on the inside surface
2. toast in a pan
3. add cherry tomatoes to a baking dish
4. add garlic cloves to the same baking dish
5. drizzle olive oil, freshly ground black pepper and salt over the garlic and tomatoes
6. bake for about 20 minutes at 200C
7. slice mozzarella
8. spread pesto on ciabatta bread bottom half
9. add mozzarella slices
10. add tomatoes
