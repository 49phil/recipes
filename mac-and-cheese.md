# Ingredients

* 50g of real cheese
* 50g of processed cheese
* salt
* 100ml milk
* macaroni
* 1 to 2tbsp of butter
* optionally: bread crumbs

# Recipe

1. start boiling the macaroni
2. grate the real cheese
3. add everything to a cooking pot
    1. 50g of grated real cheese
    2. 50g of processed cheese
    3. 1 to 2tbsp of butter depending on how rich you want it
    4. 100ml of milk
4. high heat and stir constantly otherwise the emulsion will not form!
5. if it is too thick add some milk and if it is too thin, add some processed cheese
6. optionally: add some butter to a pan and once it is melted, add some bread crumbs to brown them
7. once they are brown, set them aside on a paper towel and use them as a topping for the mac and cheese later
8. when the macaroni are done, drain them and add them to the sauce
