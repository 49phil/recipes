# Ingredients

* 1/2lb (225g) of ground beef/lamb/pork (either pure or mixture is ok)
* salt
* pepper
* 1 large onion
* 2 large ribs of celery
* 1 large carrot
* 4-5 cloves of garlic
* 1/4cup (50g) of tomato paste
* 1cup dry wine (either white or red)
* 2cups chicken stock
* 1cup milk
* optionally: fresh parsley or sage
* optionally: 4-6ounces (120-160g) of finely diced pancetta or salt pork
* optionally: parmigiano reggiano for finish

# Recipe

1. mince the onion, celery, carrot and garlic
2. heat oil in a pan
    1. optionally: add the pancetta and cook until the fat has rendered off
3. add the meat
4. season lightly with salt and pepper
5. break it up with a spoon until well browned (roughly 7 minutes)
6. add onion, celery, carrot and garlic, constantly stirring until they are softened but not browned (roughly 5 minutes)
7. add the tomato paste and cook until fragrant and there is a fond at the bottom of the pan
8. add the white wine to deglaze the pan
9. when the wine has fully reduced, add the stock and milk and combine
10. bring to a boil, then reduce heat for a very light simmer
11. stir now and again until the sauce is rich, thick and emulsified
    1. if fat forms a reddish slick on top, keep cooking until it re-emulsifies into the sauce
12. serve with pasta and finish with freshly grated parmigiano reggiano
