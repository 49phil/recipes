# Ingredients

* 200g salmon filet
* 150g feta cheese
* 400g cherry tomatoes
* 3 garlic cloves
* 2tbsp olive oil
* salt
* black pepper
* oregano
* chili flakes
* pasta of your choice

# Recipe

1. add salmon, feta cheese, cherry tomatoes and garlic cloves to a baking dish
2. add olive oil
3. add seasonings
4. preheat oven to 180C
5. bake for 30 minutes
6. start boiling your pasta so that it is done by the time you take the baking dish out of the oven
7. mix the ingredients from the baking dish together with a fork
8. add the pasta to it
