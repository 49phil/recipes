# Ingredients

* chicken breast
* salt
* pepper
* garlic powder
* parmigiano reggiano
* 3 eggs
* panko breadcrumbs
* flour
* 2L of peanut oil

# Recipe

1. butterfly the breasts
2. stamp them with a mullet
3. salt the chicken and pepper them
4. prepare the breading station
5. mix flour, garlic powder, pepper and salt
6. crack open the 3 eggs in their own bowl
7. mix panko and parmesan in a third one
8. use the dry hand, wet hand technique and put the chicken in the flour, then the egg wash and then the bread crumbs
9. optionally: put the chicken into the egg wash again and then the bread crumbs again for a double crust
10. fry them at 350F (176C) in peanut oil
11. get them out when they're golden brown
