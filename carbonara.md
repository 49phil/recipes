# Ingredients

* 4 ounces (114g) of cheese
  * typically pecorino romano
  * you can balance it out with parmigiano reggiano
* 1/2lb (225g) of guanciale or pancetta
* 4 eggs
* black pepper
* salt

# Recipe

1. add 3 eggs and 1 egg yolk to a bowl and mix them
2. add 4 ounces of grated cheese and mix it together
3. add freshly ground black pepper
4. cut guanciale into sizable chunks
5. add guanciale to cold pan and set it to medium heat
6. start boiling water in a pot for the pasta
7. once the guanciale is fully cooked but not too crispy, kill the heat
8. add the pasta to the pot
9. once the pasta is done, dump the pasta into the pan with the guanciale
10. add the egg mixture and mix fast
11. taste for seasoning, add salt if needed
