# Ingredients

* salt
* pepper
* new york strip steak
* shallots
* butter
* white wine
* beef broth

# Recipe

1. cut the very thick membrane which was attached to the fat cap
2. season the meat on both sides generously with salt and black pepper
3. start on high heat, add oil and clarified butter
4. once the fats shimmer, add the steak to the pan
5. once the steak is sizzling reduce the heat to medium
6. cook it for 4 minutes per side for medium rare
7. once the steak is done, turn off the heat
8. add some minced shallots
9. once the shallots are golden, add some white wine to deglaze and turn heat back to high
10. add some beef broth
11. reduce according to how sick you want the sauce
