# Ingredients

* 2 cups of flour
* 1 tablespoon baking powder
* 1 teaspoon fine salt
* 1 1/2 cups (300g) heavy cream
* 2-3 tablespoons of melted butter

# Recipe

1. mix the dough until it becomes shaggy
2. dust your working surface with flour and put the dough on top of it
3. keep folding the dough (preferably with a bench scraper)
4. roll it out until its about half an inch thick
5. put the cut out pieces on a baking sheet
6. brush the tops with melted butter
7. preheat oven to 500F (260C)
8. bake at 500F (260C) for about 10-12 minutes or golden brown
9. brush them again with melted butter
