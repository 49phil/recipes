# Ingredients

* pasta
* kosher salt
* unsalted butter
* optional: black pepper

# Recipe

1. put water in a pot with only as much as to cover the pasta with it
2. salt the water
3. once the water is boiling, cook the pasta in it according to the manufacturer's instructions
4. once the pasta is done, grab 1/2 cup of the pasta water and put it aside
5. drain the pasta
6. put the pasta back into the empty but still hot pot
7. add unsalted butter
8. add 1/4 cup of pasta water
9. add kosher salt
10. mix everything together until it is creamy
11. optionally: add black pepper
