# Ingredients

* 1 tablespoon (9g) active dry yeast
* 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons (139g) water
* 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons (139g) whole milk
* 4 cups (480g) bread flour
* 1 tablespoon (13g) granulated sugar
* 1 teaspoon (6g) fine sea salt
* 2 tablespoons (28g) gently melted butter or canola oil

# Recipe

1. mix milk and water
2. heat them up to 95-100F or 35-37C
3. then add dry yeast and mix it together with a fork
4. let it sit for 10 minutes
5. mix the flour, sugar and salt and mix those together
6. add yeast mixture
7. add gently melted butter (if it's too hot the yeast will die)
8. mix it with your hands until a shaggy dough forms
9. now knead it into itself for 5 to 10 minutes until a smooth dough forms
10. lightly grease a bowl
11. round the dough and put it into the bowl
12. cover the bowl with a damp towel and let it poof for at least an hour
13. when getting the dough out, punch it to get the air out
14. roll the dough out until it's about 1/2 of an inch
15. dust a baking sheet with flour
16. cut out pieces with a 3 inch circular cutter (around 7 to 7.5 cm)
17. put the cut out pieces on the baking sheet
18. cover the baking sheet with an equal size inverted one or a damp towel
19. let them poof for another 30 to 60 minutes
20. grease a pan with butter
21. medium heat for 5 to 7 minutes per side
22. put the finished muffins on a wire rack to cool
