# Ingredients

* 500g wheat flour
* 2packs dry yeast
* 6g salt
* 2tbsp sugar
* 1tsp (5g) baking soda

# Recipe

1. activate yeast in water
2. combine flour and salt
3. add yeast and water mixture to flour
4. combine until it gets too sticky
5. knead it for 10 minutes with your hands or until dough is smooth
6. lightly grease a bowl and add the dough to it
7. cover and cold ferment in the fridge for 24 hours
8. use about 120g of dough per bagel, roll it out to 25cm and lay it around your hand to make the form
9. make sure to pinch the ends together and lay them on a baking sheet
10. do so with all of your dough and let them rest for another 10 minutes
11. add water to a bowl
12. add baking soda to water
13. add sugar to water
14. bring water to a boil
15. boil each bagel in the water for about 1 minute on each side
16. after cooking each bagel, let them cool for about 20 minutes
17. pre-heat an over to 250C
18. bake the bagels for around 20 minutes
