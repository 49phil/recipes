# Ingredients

* 1.5lb (680g) of raspberries
* 50g sugar
* lemon juice

# Recipe

1. rinse the raspberries
2. add ingredients to a pot
    1. raspberries
    2. sugar
    3. couple of teaspoons of lemon juice
    4. 0 to 4 tablespoons of water depending on how thin you want the sauce
3. cook on medium heat (we do not want it to cook) until the raspberries have given up their liquid
4. strain through a sieve
5. cool in the fridge
