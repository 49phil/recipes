# Ingredients

* 1/2 asian/korean/nashi pear
* 3 cloves of garlic
* 1/2 yellow onion
* 1 spring onion
* 3tbsp brown sugar
* 1tsp black pepper
* 80ml soy sauce
* 3tbsp sesame oil
* 1/2 yellow onion for marinading the meat later

# Recipe

1. add ingredients to a food processor and blend together
2. add the marinade and 1/2 of a chopped yellow onion to the meat
3. let it marinade for 30 minutes in the fridge
