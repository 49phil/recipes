# Ingredients

* 4oz of cream cheese (Frischkäse mit Doppelrahmstufe in Germany)
* 4tbsp (57g) of softened butter
* 1tsp vanilla extract
* between 1 to 4 cups (120 to 480g) of powdered sugar

# Recipe

1. add softened butter bowl
2. add room temperature cream cheese
3. add vanilla extract
4. mix with a hand mixer
5. once everything is mixed, light and fluffy, add 1cup of powdered sugar to start
6. mix with a hand mixer until it is fully incorporated, starting on low
7. once it starts to come together, you can increase mixing speed making sure to get it smooth
8. taste it and add more powdered sugar to adjust
9. it will firm up nicely in the fridge, but is easier to spread at room temperature
