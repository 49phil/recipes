# Ingredients

* 1 can of peeled or precrushed tomatoes
* 1 onion or shallot
* black pepper
* dried oregano
* chili flakes
* 1 teaspoon of salt
* 3 garlic cloves
* optionally: 2 or 3 stems of basil
* optionally: rine of parmigiano reggiano

# Recipe

1. put olive oil in a pot
2. add 3 minced garlic cloves and fry them at medium heat
3. add half of a finely minced onion/shallot
4. add several big cranks of black pepper
5. add a teaspoon of kosher salt
6. add dried oregano
7. add chili flakes
8. stir it around for roughly 5 minutes until the onions turn translucent
9. add the peeled tomatoes to the sauce and cut the tomatoes with a spoon (or use precrushed)
10. optionally: add the rine to the sauce (you have to later fish it out)
11. optionally: add two or three big stems of basil (you have to later fish those out)
12. let the sauce simmer for 15 minutes on low and stir it around every once in a while
13. salt to taste
