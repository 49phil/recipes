# Ingredients

* brown mushrooms
* marsala wine
* chicken stock
* chicken breasts
* parsley
* salt
* black pepper
* eggs
* optonal: lemon juice

# Recipe

1. pound out the chicken breasts
2. season the chicken on both sides
    1. salt
    2. black pepper
3. bread the chicken and refrigerate for later
4. melt butter in a pan on high heat
5. add thinly sliced mushrooms
6. season with salt
7. once the mushrooms are nice and browned, reduce heat to medium and add flour
8. add marsala wine
9. optionally: add a couple of teaspoons of lemon juice
10. add chicken stock and raise heat to medium high
11. add freshly ground pepper
12. cook until it has the desired thickness
13. add parsley and keep the sauce hot until the chicken is cooked
14. now take the chicken from the fridge and cook it in olive oil
