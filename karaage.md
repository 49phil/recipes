# Ingredients

* 1 large garlic clove
* 1tbsp finely grated ginger
* 3tbsp soy sauce
* 3tbsp sake
* 1tbsp mirin
* 1/8tsp sesame oil
* 1/4tsp ground black pepper
* a pinch of cayenne
* 1/2tsp white sugar
* 1/4tsp kosher salt
* 1 1/4lb (560g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs
* 1cup potato starch (alternatively use cornstarch)
* 1l canola oil

# Recipe

1. grate the garlic into a bowl
2. grate the ginger into the same bowl
3. add soy sauce
4. add sake
5. add mirin
6. add sesame oil
7. add black pepper
8. add cayenne
9. add white sugar
10. add kosher salt
11. mix together
12. cut the chicken thighs into 2in pieces
13. add chicken to marinade and make sure all pieces are coated
14. marinate in fridge for 1 to 10 hours
15. bread the chicken in potato starch and shake of the excess
16. transfer to wire rack
17. if any spots are bright white, rub those off since they will appear as white patches after frying
18. once all pieces are breaded, transfer to fridge for 15 to 30 minutes
19. fry in 350F (175C) for 3 to 4 minutes
