# Ingredients

* 0.25oz active dry yeast
* 1/4cup warm water
* 1/2cup unsalted butter
* 1cup milk
* 3tbsp white sugar
* 3 3/4cups all purpose flour
* 1 1/2tsp kosher salt / 1tsp fine salt
* 1 large egg yolk
* 1/4cup butter, melted

# Recipe

1. sprinkle yeast into warm water
2. stir and let it sit for 15 to 20 minutes or until foamy
3. place butter in saucepan
4. add milk and white sugar
5. cook at medium heat and stir occasionaly until butter and white sugar dissolved
6. pour into mixing bowl and let it cool until just very warm to the touch
7. add flour, salt, yeast mixture and egg to the mixing bowl
8. mix gently with a spatula until it comes together and forms a thick and wet batter
9. add remaining flour and mix with your fingers until dough comes together into a soft, sticky ball
10. cover and let rise for about 1 hour
11. transfer dough to floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until smooth
12. grease a bowl with butter and place the dough inside
13. cover and let it rise for 30 minutes
14. preheat oven to 375F (190C)
15. transfer dough to work surface and press it into 1in thick circle
16. use a pizza cutter to cut the dough into 12 equal wedges
17. starting at the larger end of one wedge, roll toward the point while stretching the ends out for the first few inches
18. pinch and press the point into the doug to seal and place seam-side down on a baking sheet
19. repeat with remaining dough
20. let rolls sit for 15 to 20 minutes (or 45 minutes for lighter, airier rolls)
21. bake in the center of the preheated oven until they turn light golden brown, about 20 to 25 minutes
22. remove from the oven and brush the still hot rolls with melted butter
