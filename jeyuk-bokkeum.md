# Ingredients

* 600g pork belly
* 2tbsp gochugaru
* 2tbsp gochujang
* 2tbsp soy sauce
* 2tbsp garlic
* 2tbsp apple juice
* 2tbsp soju or mirin
* 2tbsp sugar
* 1/2 yellow onion
* 1 spring onion
* 1 jalapeno
* 1/2tsp roasted sesame seeds

# Recipe

1. cut the meat into small pieces and add it to a bowl
2. add minced garlic to the same bowl
3. add gochujang
4. add gochugaru
5. add apple juice
6. add soy sauce
7. add mirin
8. add sugar
9. combine everything
10. cut 1/2 yellow onion, the spring onion and jalapeno into strips
11. cook both onions on high heat for about a minute
12. add the meat and cook for about 5 minutes or until it is cooked through
13. add jalapeno and cook for another minute
14. garnish with sesame seeds
